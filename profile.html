Dipen Gada & Associates, popularly known as DGA, began as a very modest interior design firm.
Gradually with time and every project accomplished, DGA evolved from an exclusive interior design
firm to a civil and architectural planning firm and attained the position as one of the respectable
and admired firms of Baroda.Since its inception in 1993, DGA has made its presence felt through
innovative, minimalistic and timeless designs. The principal designer and founder of the firm,
Mr. Dipen Gada holds a Bachelors degree in Civil Engineering from M.S University, Vadodara, Gujarat.
Initially, he strove for knowledge and exposure which boarded him on a path of self-learning and
grasp experience for resources around. Intense travelling, study and seminars helped him create a
boutique identity of his practice.
